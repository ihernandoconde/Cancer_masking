Privacy Policy

Disclaimer: This website was created as part of a university project. It is in no way intended to be used by the general public. This is just a draft of a privacy policy. It is not a legally binding document. Please do not take it seriously.

This privacy policy is used to inform you of how “Synapse systems” will use and protect your data. We can change this policy at any given time, so we advise you to keep an eye out. When you visit our website, you may be asked if you opt for any data to be collected.

Opt-out

No data will be collected nor stored.

Opt_in

What data do we collect

If you decide to opt-in for data sharing, we may collect the following data:

  • Your IP address
  • Your email address
  • Your mammograms

Why do we collect them

We are collecting them to improve and provide our services. This is because:

  • Your IP address – it is used to see which demographics use our website and estimate the usefulness of our services.
  • Your email address – to send surveys. This is used to as a performance assessment. By completing the survey, and after consulting with your doctor, you will be able to send us feedback about the performance of our website. This information will then be used to improve our services.
  • Your mammograms – it will be used to keep training the CNN responsible for the breast density estimation, making sure it is always up to a good performance.

Safeguarding and Data Protection

All the information collected (IP addresses, email addresses and mammograms) will be deleted 3 years after the collection. The data will not be shared with any third parties. If you require you collected data to be deleted prior to the 3-year storage period, you may contact us in our office and provide the email address used when opting in. We assure their deletion upon request.
